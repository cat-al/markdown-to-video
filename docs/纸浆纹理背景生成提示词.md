用于 GPT-Image-2（或其他 AI 图片生成工具）生成视频背景纹理图。

## 提示词

> A seamless high-resolution texture of handmade paper, shot from directly above with flat, even lighting. The paper has a warm ivory/cream base color (#FAF3E9). Scattered across the surface are short plant fiber fragments — each fiber is about 5-15 millimeters long, thin and slightly curved. They appear in moderate density: clearly visible and giving the paper a handmade character, but not so dense that they overwhelm the surface. The fibers vary in tone from pale golden to light brown. A few tiny dark specks (pulp impurities) are also present. The paper has a gentle uneven thickness creating a soft, natural mottled tone variation. No text, no patterns, no objects — pure paper texture only. The overall feel is warm, organic, and artisanal, with the fibers adding tactile character without competing with content placed on top. Resolution: 1920×1080 pixels.
    
## 使用说明

1. 将上面的提示词输入 GPT-Image-2，尺寸设为 **1920×1080**
2. 生成后将图片保存为 `.codebuddy/skills/markdown-to-html/assets/paper-texture-bg.png`
3. 生成 HTML 时，将图片以 base64 data URI 内联到 CSS 中（因为 skill 资源不在运行时路径上），或在生成脚本中将图片复制到输出目录旁

当前已生成的背景图位于：`.codebuddy/skills/markdown-to-html/assets/paper-texture-bg.png`

## 调整方向

如果初次生成效果不满意，可以尝试以下变体：

- **纤维更密集**：在提示词中加 `densely packed fibers covering most of the surface`
- **纤维更稀疏**：加 `sparse, minimal fibers with large areas of clean paper`
- **更粗糙/原始**：加 `rough, unrefined, rustic handmade paper with visible pulp chunks`
- **更干净/精致**：加 `refined, smooth handmade paper with only delicate thin fibers`
- **偏暖/偏黄**：改 base color 为 `warm golden cream (#F0E6D0)`
- **偏冷/偏白**：改 base color 为 `cool off-white (#F5F0EA)`
- **加入花瓣**：加 `with tiny dried flower petals pressed into the paper`
